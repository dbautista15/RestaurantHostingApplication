Goal: Manage assigned tables and provide service
Login as Waiter

As a waiter, I want to log in so I can see my assigned tables and parties.
View Assigned Tables

As a waiter, I want to see which tables are assigned to me so I know whom to serve.
View Party Sizes

As a waiter, I want to see the size of each party at my tables to prepare accordingly.
Mark Table as Available

As a waiter, I want to mark a table as available when a party leaves so it can be reassigned.
Receive Notifications (Nice to Have)

As a waiter, I want to receive notifications when a new party is assigned to me so I can greet them promptly.
